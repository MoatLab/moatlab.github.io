Prof. Huaicheng Li received the **NSF CAREER Award** (CNS-2339901), *Rethinking System Stack for the Load-Store I/O Era*. The five-year sole-PI award (2024-2029, $676,549) supports research on memory and storage systems for the load/store I/O era.
